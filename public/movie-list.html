The Best Movies According to Richard

My Favourite Movies

The Menu

Thriller with a tad bit of mystery, and unironically not so great when you have a big appetite.

Edge Of Tomorrow

Groundhog Day scenarios are always fun watches to me. Especially when combined with amazing fight scenes, and the awesome mecha suits the soldiers are given to fight with.

Captain America: The First Avenger

Underdog, Steve Rodgers, is a scrawny guy that eventually shows how a good person is all you need to be to make a difference... along with a massive steroid boost designed to turn humans into super soldiers. But to be fair it was only given to him to make sure a good person was going to be using it.
